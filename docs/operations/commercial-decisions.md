# Open Commercial Decisions

These inputs remain unresolved and require approval before a customer commitment:
pricing/billing basis, licensing terms, SLO targets, support hours/tiers, incident
notification windows, service limits/quotas, backup retention and RPO/RTO, regions/data
residency, model pass-through costs and currency rates, legal retention/deletion terms,
and customer exit/export obligations.

Implemented visibility is limited to safe technical facts: token usage, corpus/storage
counts, latency, job/webhook status, dependency health, versions, and audit history.
These measurements are not invoices, contractual limits, or guarantees.
